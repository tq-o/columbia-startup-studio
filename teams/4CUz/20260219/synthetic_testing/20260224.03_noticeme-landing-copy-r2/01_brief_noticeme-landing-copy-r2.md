# 01 Brief: noticeme-landing-copy

## 1. What we're testing
Landing page messaging for NoticeMe, focused on reducing unintentional scrolling through social accountability (not app blocking).

## 2. Who is seeing this
Primary arrivals: Instagram/TikTok ad clicks from student audiences. Secondary arrivals: organic search around screen time and focus.

## 3. Persona buckets (5 x 12 = 60)
- self_control_fatigued: knows the issue, repeats failed attempts, emotionally tired of restarting
- social_motivated: responds to peer visibility and light public accountability
- privacy_guarded: concerned about data sharing and social exposure
- deletion_rebounder: repeatedly deletes social apps then reinstalls
- skeptical_optimizer: analytical evaluator who needs mechanism clarity and proof

For each bucket in audience.json: include behavior summary, in-their-voice quote, and prevalence.

## 4. Product description
NoticeMe is a social accountability app that helps students reduce unintentional scrolling by making screen-time patterns visible to trusted peers, without blocking social apps.

## 5. Pricing
Assumed for test context: free tier + $5.99/month premium insights.

## 6. Copy skeleton
Hero, problem framing, why blockers fail, social accountability mechanism, privacy controls, social proof, pricing, CTA.

## 7. Evaluation criteria
Use standard schema with mandatory clarity_response. Evaluate resonance, intent, conversion confidence, price perception, strongest lines, objections, dealbreakers.

## 8. Stopping criteria
Minimum 5 rounds, stop if conversion confidence agree+ > 50%, max 10 rounds, or convergence across two consecutive rounds.
